Nanopore Sequencing

Nanopore Sequencing is a third-generation sequencing technology provided by Oxford Nanopore Technologies (ONT)

Long-Read 

The fact that it is a long-read sequencing makes it possible to sequence RNA/DNAs in their full length.

Direct RNA/DNA

By using Nanopore sequencing, we can capture the nucleic acids in their native form. Hence, we can detect RNA modifications.

RNA Modifications

We are investigating chemical alterations in the RNA molecules

RNA Modification Machinery

Exploring the RNA modification machinery proteins in terms of phylogenetic and expression patterns

(Begik et al, 2020. "Integrative analyses of the RNA modification machinery reveal tissue- and cancer-specific signatures", BioRxiv)

Detection of RNA modifications using Direct RNA Sequencing (ONT)

Taking advantage of Direct RNA sequencing technology in order to detect RNA modifications 
(Liu & Begik et al., 2019. "Accurate detection of m6A RNA modifications in native RNA sequences", Nature Communications)
